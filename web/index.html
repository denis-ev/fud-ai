Fud AI app,
calorie tracker.

The Fud app is a free, open-source AI calorie tracker and food scanner for iPhone and Android. Snap a photo, scan a packaged-food barcode, add a note to camera or library meals, speak your meal, or type it — bring your own AI provider, or use optional Fud AI Plus on iOS with no API setup. Track weight and body fat together, customize Home nutrient cards, set optional detailed nutrient goals, and let AI suggest those goals without changing the core calorie and macro calculator.

§ 02   Chapters

What it does, chapter by chapter.

01

Capture a meal.

Photo · Barcode · Note · Voice · Text · Manual

Snap a photo of any meal, scan a packaged-food barcode, add a note to a camera or photo-library image, scan a nutrition label, speak your lunch hands-free, type it, or enter macros manually when you already know them.

  • Native camera + photo library import on both platforms, with optional notes for extra meal context
  • Barcode lookup on iOS uses Open Food Facts when public packaged-food data is available
  • Voice input via 6 speech-to-text options with per-provider language selection
  • Smart serving units show slices, pieces, cups, ml, or similar units when the AI can infer them
  • Text and manual fallbacks for quick logging
02

AI does the math.

13 LLM providers

Bring your own key, or use optional Fud AI Plus on iOS for no-key Gemini food analysis, Deepgram voice transcription, and Coach access. BYOK lets you choose from Gemini, OpenAI, Anthropic Claude, Grok, OpenRouter, Together AI, Groq, Hugging Face, Fireworks, DeepInfra, Mistral, Ollama (local), or any custom OpenAI-compatible endpoint.

  • Open-weight vision models out of the box: Gemma, Llama Vision, Qwen VL, Pixtral
  • OpenRouter testable on $0 credits — the openrouter/free default routes to a free vision model so you can try the integration without funding the account
  • Custom AI Instructions — optional Settings text appended to every AI request. Drop your region, diet, or brand context once instead of repeating it per meal ("I live in Germany, prefer ALDI/REWE brands" or "I'm on a bodybuilding cut")
  • Fallback Provider — opt-in second provider that auto-retries when your primary fails on overload, rate limit, or network error. Pair a paid model with a free fallback for cheap reliability
  • API keys stored in iOS Keychain (iOS) or EncryptedSharedPreferences with AES-256 (Android) — never transmitted to us
  • Switch providers and models anytime; transient overloads (503 / 429 / 529) auto-retry with backoff, and AI responses with surrounding prose or markdown fences are parsed reliably
03

Track 13 nutrients.

Macros + micros

Calories, protein, carbs, fat, sugar, fiber, saturated fat, mono-unsaturated fat, poly-unsaturated fat, cholesterol, sodium, potassium, and added sugar — adjustable serving sizes and AI-suggested units recalculate every logged entry live.

  • Personalized BMR + TDEE via Katch-McArdle / Mifflin-St Jeor
  • Auto daily targets, fully overridable, with per-macro pin / auto-balance
  • Optional nutrient goals for fiber, sugar, saturated fat, cholesterol, sodium, and potassium stay separate from calorie/protein/carbs/fat math
  • Home cards can show macros or selected detailed nutrients
  • Browse past days with the date strip; configurable week start and optional latest-first food log sorting
04

Talk to your Coach.

Multi-turn AI chat

A dedicated tab where the AI sees your full profile, weight history, food log, current date/timezone, richer meal details, and a thermodynamic forecast. Ask "what's my expected weight in 30 days?" or "am I eating enough protein?" and get answers grounded in your own data.

  • Memory persists locally across sessions
  • Goal-aware prompt chips for Lose / Gain / Maintain
  • Long-press any reply to copy
05

Glance from anywhere.

Home Screen + Lock Screen widgets

iOS ships five widget families across Home Screen and Lock Screen — circular, rectangular, inline, small, medium — with both Calorie and Protein widgets. Android ships Calorie and Protein Glance widgets in two sizes each (small + medium). All update the moment you log a meal.

  • Pink-gradient calorie ring with macro progress at a glance
  • Above-the-clock circular and below-the-clock rectangular on iOS Lock Screen
  • Auto-roll over at midnight without opening the app
06

Sync with Apple Health & Health Connect.

Bidirectional · iOS + Android

iOS writes 12 nutrition types per meal plus body measurements (weight, height, body fat) to Apple Health and reads weight, height, body fat, date of birth, and sex back into your profile. Android does the same through Health Connect — also pulling in external weight samples from Samsung Health, Fitbit, Withings, and any other Health Connect–compatible app.

  • Background observers for real-time bi-sync
  • UUID-tagged samples for safe per-entry deletion (no orphans)
  • Delete All Data is local-only — Apple Health / Health Connect data is never touched
07

Speaks fifteen languages.

Auto-localized

English, Arabic, Azerbaijani, Chinese (Simplified), Dutch, French, German, Hindi, Italian, Japanese, Korean, Portuguese (Brazil), Romanian, Russian, Spanish — auto-selected by your phone's system language on both iOS and Android. Voice input can also be guided separately per STT provider.

08

Make it yours.

Theme colors + matching icons

Choose Fud Pink, red, orange, green, mint, teal, blue, or purple in Settings. The accent color updates across tabs, buttons, rings, charts, and the iOS app icon / Android launcher icon.

§ 05   FAQ

Fud AI app, plainly answered.

Is Fud AI the Fud app?

Yes. Fud AI, Fud AI app, Fud app, and AI Fud all point to the same open-source calorie tracker for iPhone and Android.

Can it work as an AI food scanner?

Yes. Log food from camera photos, barcode lookup, photo-library images, photos with notes, nutrition labels, voice, text, saved meals, or manual entry.

Does it replace a macro tracker?

It tracks calories, protein, carbs, and fat, plus detailed nutrients like fiber, sugar, saturated fat, cholesterol, sodium, potassium, mono-fat, poly-fat, and added sugar.

Can I use it without an API key?

Bring Your Own Key is always available. On iOS, optional Fud AI Plus adds no-key Gemini food analysis, Deepgram speech transcription, and Coach access with daily limits.

Does it sync with Health apps?

Yes. Fud AI supports Apple Health on iPhone and Health Connect on Android for body measurements and meal nutrition when you explicitly enable sync.

Is it private and open source?

Yes. There is no Fud AI account, no cloud sync, no in-app tracking, and no ads. The MIT-licensed source code is available on GitHub.

    Become tester

    Install from Play Store

    Once provisioned, open the Play Store listing on your Android phone and install.
