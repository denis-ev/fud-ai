Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

Fud AI ("we", "our", "the app") is an open-source iOS app developed by Apoorv Darshan. This privacy policy explains how your information is handled.

Information You Provide

  • Name, gender, date of birth, height, weight, and body fat percentage (entered during onboarding)
  • Food photos taken within the app
  • Food entries and nutrition data you log
  • Weight log entries
  • Dietary preferences and fitness goals
  • AI provider API keys (stored in iOS Keychain)

How We Use Your Information

  • AI Food Analysis: Food photos, text descriptions, and voice input are sent to your chosen AI provider (e.g. Google Gemini, OpenAI, Anthropic Claude, etc.) for nutritional analysis. You bring your own API key. We do not proxy or intercept these requests.
  • Local Storage: All food entries, weight entries, user profile data, and food photos are stored locally on your device. Nothing leaves your device except AI analysis requests.
  • API Key Storage: Your API keys are stored in iOS Keychain — encrypted hardware storage accessible only to the app on your device.
  • Apple Health: If you enable Apple Health integration, the app writes body mass, height, and body fat percentage to HealthKit, and reads body mass, height, body fat percentage, date of birth, and biological sex from HealthKit.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not use any third-party analytics, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs
  • We do not track your behavior, location, or browsing activity
  • We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to any third parties
  • We do not operate any servers — all data stays on your device
  • We do not require an account, sign-in, or cloud sync

Open Source

Fud AI is open source under the MIT License. You can review the full source code at github.com/apoorvdarshan/fud-ai.

Data Retention

  • All data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app
  • You can delete all data at any time using the "Delete All Data" option in Profile settings

Children's Privacy

Fud AI is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
